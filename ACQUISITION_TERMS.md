# Verity News Stack: Acquisition Terms

This document outlines the terms for the Full IP Acquisition (Tier 3) of the Verity News Stack.

### 1. Assets Included
The acquisition includes the total transfer of:
- The `verity-hq` GitHub Organization.
- All associated repositories: `verity-api`, `verity-mobile`, and `verity-dashboard`.
- All Intellectual Property, copyrights, and branding associated with "Verity News Stack."

### 2. Prior Licenses (Grandfathering)
The Buyer acknowledges that the Verity News Stack has been sold as a "Source Available" product. The Buyer agrees to honor all Standard and Agency licenses granted via GitHub Sponsors prior to the Transfer Date. The Buyer may not initiate copyright claims or "takedown" requests against these existing authorized users.

### 3. Handover Process
1. **Verification:** Potential Buyer emails `fulleni@hotmail.com` to confirm availability.
2. **Payment:** Buyer completes the $1,999 sponsorship.
3. **Agreement:** Seller provides a formal IP Assignment Agreement for digital signature.
4. **Transfer:** Upon signature, Seller initiates the "Transfer Ownership" process for the GitHub Organization to the Buyer's account.

### 4. No Ongoing Obligation
Upon transfer, the original developer (Seller) is released from all maintenance, support, and update obligations. The project is acquired "As-Is."
